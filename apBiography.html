Brief Biography

Fr. Elias Nwaodu was born into the family of late Mr. Canisius John Igwe and Late Mrs. Florence Chijiago Nwaodu of Umudibia Umualumaku Ehime, Mbano L.G.A, Imo State. He is the second son and the last child of the family of seven. Fr. Elias was ordained a Priest of the Catholic Diocese of Okigwe by His Grace Most Rev. Dr. Augustine Kasujja, the apostolic Nuncio to Nigeria on 24, September 2011 at Immaculate Conception Cathedral, Okigwe, Imo State. After his ordination, he has worked in St. Charles Parish, Umunumo, Ugonna Parish, Lowa Ihitte/Uboma, St. Michael's Parish, Umukabia, St. Brigid Parish, Ijesha and currently in St. Catherine Parish, Alakuko as the Associate Parish Priest.
