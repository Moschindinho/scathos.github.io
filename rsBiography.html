Brief Biography

Rev. Sr. Justinamary Onyeanwusi is of the religious institute of the Eucharistic Heart of Jesus Sisters (E.H.J). They were founded in 1943 by Archbishop Leo Hale Taylor of blessed memory. They are an institute of Pontifical Rite. She had her first profession of vows in 2001 and perpetual vow in 2008. She is currently working in one of their schools....Eucharistic Heart Of Jesus Model School, Meiran and St. Catherine Parish, Alakuko as the Parish Sister.
